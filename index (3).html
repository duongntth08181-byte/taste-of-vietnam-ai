About Taste of Vietnam

Taste of Vietnam is a student-built project showcasing Vietnamese culinary heritage with AI-powered exploration. Our mission is to connect users—locals and visitors alike—with authentic recipes, regional insights, and cultural stories behind each dish.

  • Curated regional recipes and histories
  • Interactive AI that explains culture and cooking
  • Bilingual content for accessibility

Our Approach

We combine design, curated culinary knowledge, and AI to create an approachable learning experience. The chatbot is tuned to provide concise, accurate historical context as well as practical cooking tips.
